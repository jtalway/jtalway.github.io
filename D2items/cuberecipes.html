Armor = Armor refers to any sort of armor, including helms, boots, belts, gloves and shields, not just body armor.
Upgrade Version = This formula changes the base item type from the normal to exceptional version. Unique item bonuses remain the same; only the item it's based on is upgraded. This can be used to upgrade Ethereal Items also. They will continue to be Ethereal after they are upgraded. Also if the item is socketed, then jewels or runes and their mods will also remain.
Socketed Item = The number of sockets created will vary. The item must be normal and unsocketed. Low-quality items and superior items do not work. The item gets sockets, randomly. However, the number of sockets is restricted by the maximum number of sockets that base item with that ilvl can have.
