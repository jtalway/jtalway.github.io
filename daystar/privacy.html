Daystar Custom Signs Privacy Policy

Daystar Custom Signs values privacy and is committed to protecting your privacy while providing quality services that give you an enjoyable and safe online experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we use, share and protect the personally identifiable information that we collect about you (“Personal Information”) and the choices you have. This Privacy Policy applies to visitors to www.daystarcustomsigns.com (“Website”) and to our online customers. By visiting or using our Website, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Collection of your Personal Information

Daystar Custom Signs collects personally identifiable information when you register or order, such as your e-mail address, physical address, name, and phone number, and credit card information. Daystar Custom Signs also collects information not personally identifiable, such as zip code, city and state.

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collect by Daystar Custom Signs. This information can include your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses, templates made, path through the site, and operating system information. This information is used by Daystar Custom Signs for the operation of its services, to maintain quality of service and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Daystar Custom Signs web site.

Daystar Custom Signs encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from Daystar Custom Signs so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. Daystar Custom Signs is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the Daystar Custom Signs web site.

Use of your Personal Information

Daystar Custom Signs collects and uses your personal information to operate the Daystar Custom Signs web site and deliver the services you have requested. Daystar Custom Signs also uses your personally identifiable information from time to time to inform you of other products or services available from Daystar Custom Signs and its affiliates. Daystar Custom Signs may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered. Note that at any time you may opt out of being contacted by Daystar Custom Signs for any research or product updates, through clear links at the bottom of every email.

Daystar Custom Signs does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Daystar Custom Signs may, form time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, Daystar Custom Signs may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Daystar Custom Signs, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Daystar Custom Signs does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

Daystar Custom Signs keeps track of the web sites and pages our customers visit within and from the Daystar Custom Signs web site, in order to determine what Daystar Custom Signs services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within Daystar Custom Signs to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Daystar Custom Signs will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Daystar Custom Signs or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Daystar Custom Signs; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Daystar Custom Signs, or the public.

Use of Cookies

Daystar Custom Signs web site may use “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you register with the Daystar Custom Signs site or services, a cookie helps Daystar Custom Signs to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same Daystar Custom Signs web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Daystar Custom Signs features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Daystar Custom Signs services or Web sites you visit.

Security of your Personal Information

Daystar Custom Signs secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Daystar Custom Signs secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.

Daystar Custom Signs will not record, store, or save your Credit Card Number without your express consent and notice from Daystar Custom Signs.

Changes to this Statement

Daystar Custom Signs will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. Daystar Custom Signs encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how Daystar Custom Signs is protecting your information.
