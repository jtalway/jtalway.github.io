LED Message Centers

A sign that uses computer-generated messages or some other means of changing the words. These signs also include lamps, LEDs, LCDs, or flipper matrix.

Monument Signs

A ground sign with low overall height that is not attached to a building.

Pylon Signs

A freestanding sign with a visible support structure. It may or may not be enclosed by a pole cover (an enclosure for concealing and/or decorating poles or other structural supports of a ground sign).

Channel & Dimensional Letters

A three-dimensional letter, logo, or symbol that has been cut-out, cast, fabricated, or molded from material such as metal or plastic, that may include a light source.
